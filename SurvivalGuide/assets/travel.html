Travelling around Newcastle is easy with various ways to travel including Bus and Metro.

BUS

There are a number of bus operators that provide bus services who also have discounted tickets for students travelling regularly on their bus. More information on these tickets can be found on their website where the student tickets can be bought online.

Main Bus Stations:

  • Haymarket Bus Station
  • Monument Bus Station

You can use this website to guide you on your route on bus and also has details on timetables of buses.

Metro

The Metro is an easy and quick way to travel around in the North East. Daily, weekly, or termly tickets are available. You may find the Metro Stations nearby on this website.


For more information on tickets and prices, click here.

Travelling to Newcastle

Airport

The Newcastle International Airport is just 7 miles from Newcastle University.

Taxis

There are Official Airport Taxis that will pick you up and drop you off outside the terminal. For more details, click here.

Any other booked taxis will pick you up and drop you off across the road from the terminal. You can also get an un-booked taxi here.

Metro

You can also travel by Metro which can be taken from the Metro Station within the airport which takes about 25 minutes to reach Newcastle City Centre.

Bus

Buses to Newcastle City Centre can be caught right outside the airport.
