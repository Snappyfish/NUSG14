Fire Information

Do you know what to do in a fire?

  • Don’t ignore you fire alarm, you may think its a drill but one day it may not be
  • Do you know where your extinguishers are?
  • Shut your doors when leaving or sleeping?
    • The extra time a closed door can give you in a fire is essential
  • Make sure you know your escape route
    • More than one, some may be inaccessible

Make sure you know how to contact the fire brigade â€“ Dial 999

Fire prevention

  • Electric blankets, remember to turn them off.
  • Turn off all appliances when leaving or sleeping
  • Turn off laptops, phones or other devices that may be left on charge overnight
  • Double check that oven, did you turn it off?
  • Do you use candles?
  • Remember to extinguish these before leaving the room and going to sleep
  • Keep them away from flammable objects

Safety tips (your recommended)

  • Don’t go into Fenham or Biker after dark
  • Keep out of those back alleys unless accompanied by a group
  • Keep an eye on your drinks
    • Keep your drinks covered, don’t leave them where you can’t see them
    • Be careful about what you accept from others
  • Don’t advertise your stuff
  • Don’t wave your gadgets about keep them in your bags
