CLEARING!

Were you unsuccessful in securing a place at University after your A level results day?

If your application to Newcastle University was via UCAS then you can check vacancies for courses. If any of the courses interest you then you can phone the hotline and find out if you may join that course.

Hotline:
Telephone: (0191) 222 6000

Clearing is highly competitive hence it’s wise to check the website in the fortnight leading up to the A level results day to find out about process and how to prepare.
Clearing will be open once the A-Level results are released.
