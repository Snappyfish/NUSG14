The city of Newcastle offers brilliant education in the form of Newcastle University, but people also flock to Newcastle for its spectacular nightlife.
The city is renowned for its nights out, and we can guide you through the sticky floors and spilled Jagerbombs to find a night you’ll (vaguely) remember!

Popular Venues

Digital

Newcastle’s biggest and perhaps most famous club Digital, offers nights on Monday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday, each with different themed music that you can dance to, drink to, or just bop your head to. Each night features drinks deals so you don’t have to break the bank to have a good time.

Location: Life Centre
Editor’s Choice: LOVE on Saturdays

Tiger Tiger

With a few branches across the country, Tiger is an established venue, and for good reason which the Newcastle location does not disappoint. With 7 differently themed rooms, Tiger creates a different feel in every room, with the White Room being at the centre of the night.

Next to the Gate
Editor’s Choice: Animal on Wednesdays

Tup Tup Palace

Tup Tup claims to be Newcastle’s premier nightclub, and you’d have trouble arguing against it. The club oozes quality in its music, furnishings and drinks. Drinks prices are on the more expensive side within Newcastle, however there are always offers to take advantage of.

Next to the Gate
Editor’s Choice: Animal on Wednesdays
